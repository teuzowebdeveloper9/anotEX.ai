# PR: Interactive quiz, clickable timestamps, and content export

## Context

This PR consolidates the latest transcription-study experience workstream, aligned with the roadmap in [ai-docs/06-features-roadmap.md](/home/teuzothedev/work/anotEx.ai/ai-docs/06-features-roadmap.md).

Delivered planned scope:

1. Interactive Quiz
2. Clickable Timestamps
3. Content Export (PDF/TXT/Anki)

These three initiatives were planned as quick wins or high-impact low-to-medium effort deliveries, focused on improving retention, active review, and the practical usefulness of AI-generated study content.

## Summary

This package significantly improves the post-transcription study flow in anotEX.ai. The app now supports:

- active knowledge review through automatically generated quizzes
- precise transcript navigation through clickable time-based segments
- practical content export for studying outside the platform

Together, these changes turn the transcription from a passive artifact into a more interactive, navigable, and reusable study surface.

## What Was Delivered

### 1. Interactive Quiz

Implementation of the quiz experience based on the `quiz` study material already generated by the backend.

Includes:

- new authenticated `/quiz` route
- completed transcription listing with available quiz data
- navigation to the quiz tab inside the transcription page
- quiz player with per-question progression
- immediate correct/incorrect feedback
- per-question explanation
- final score screen with retry support

Main technical areas:

- `frontend/src/pages/quiz/ui/QuizPage.tsx`
- `frontend/src/widgets/quiz-player/ui/QuizPlayer.tsx`
- `frontend/src/app/App.tsx`
- `frontend/src/widgets/sidebar/ui/Sidebar.tsx`

### 2. Clickable Timestamps in the Transcript

Upgrade of the transcription pipeline to support time segments and contextual audio playback directly from transcript text.

Includes:

- Groq Whisper transcription using `verbose_json`
- capture of `segments` with `start`, `end`, and `text`
- persistence of segments in the entity and database
- segmented rendering in the frontend
- click on timestamp to seek to the exact audio position
- active segment highlighting while audio playback progresses
- audio player and error-handling improvements

Main technical areas:

- `backend/src/modules/transcription/infrastructure/providers/groq-whisper.provider.impl.ts`
- `backend/src/modules/transcription/domain/entities/transcription.entity.ts`
- `backend/src/modules/transcription/domain/use-cases/process-transcription.use-case.ts`
- `backend/src/modules/transcription/infrastructure/repositories/transcription.repository.impl.ts`
- `supabase/migrations/20260318000000_add_segments_to_transcriptions.sql`
- `frontend/src/widgets/transcription-viewer/ui/TranscriptionViewer.tsx`
- `frontend/src/pages/transcription/ui/TranscriptionPage.tsx`
- `frontend/src/shared/types/api.types.ts`

### 3. Content Export (PDF/TXT/Anki)

Added the export layer for taking study content outside the product.

Includes:

- transcript export as `.txt`
- summary export through a printable PDF flow
- flashcard export in a tab-separated format compatible with Anki import
- export CTA integrated into the transcription page

Main technical areas:

- `frontend/src/features/transcription/export/model/useExport.ts`
- `frontend/src/features/transcription/export/ui/ExportButton.tsx`
- `frontend/src/pages/transcription/ui/TranscriptionPage.tsx`

- improves retention through active review with quizzes

- improves product completeness in the post-processing journey

## Risk

Overall risk: medium.

Key attention points:

- compatibility of the `verbose_json` transcription provider response
- correct migration and retrieval of the `segments` column
- audio behavior across different browsers
- export flow depending on browser print/download behavior and permissions
